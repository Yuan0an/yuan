Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that plans can change. Below is our policy regarding cancellations and refunds:

1. General Cancellation

All cancellation requests must be made in writing or through our official contact channels.

2. Refund Schedule

Notice Period Refund Amount
30+ Days before event 80% of Down Payment
15-29 Days before event 50% of Down Payment
Less than 15 Days Non-refundable

3. Rescheduling

  • Rescheduling is allowed once, at least 14 days before the original date, subject to availability.
  • A rescheduling fee of P1,000 may apply.

4. Force Majeure

In cases of extreme weather conditions or government-mandated lockdowns, we will offer a full reschedule without additional fees.
